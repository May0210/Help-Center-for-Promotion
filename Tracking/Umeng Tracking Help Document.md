#### 1. Choose the app you are going to track in 应用管理
![image](imgs/umeng_1_choose_app.png)

#### 2. Select“添加推广计划”
![image](imgs/umeng_2_add_promoted_plan.png)

#### 3. Fill in basic information according to your demands, and the click 下一步
![image](imgs/umeng_3_basic_information.png)

#### 4. Choose “ZPLAY Ads” in “推广渠道”，and fill in other blanks according to your demands
![image](imgs/umeng_4_choose_promoted_channel.png)

#### 5. Click 确认 to save your settings
![image](imgs/umeng_5_filling_completed.png)

#### 6. After saving, copy 点击监测链接 and 曝光检测代码 in this page
![image](imgs/umeng_6_copy_link.png)

#### 7. If you need to check tracking url afterwards, you can select the app in the 应用管理 page, then you will see the form of promotion plan you have created. Click "监测" button in the right side of the form, and you will see the tracking url of your promotion plan again
![image](imgs/umeng_6.2_check_again.png)

#### 8. Click “渠道管理” in “渠道管理”
![image](imgs/umeng_7_channel_management.png)

#### 9. Fill in "zplay" in the search box and swithch on the data transmission button. Only when this button is on, ZPLAY Ads can receive install information of your app, and then can settle accounts with you correctly
![image](imgs/umeng_8_choose_zplay.png)

#### 10. In the create campaign or edit campaign page of ZPLAY Ads, fill the View-Through Tracking URL with 曝光检测代码 which is copied in step 6. And fill the Tracking URL with 点击监测链接 which is copied in step 6.
![image](imgs/umeng_9_tracking_en.png)
